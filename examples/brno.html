The Student City of Czech Republic

Erasmus+ Program in Brno

The Erasmus+ programme provides opportunities for students to study, train, gain work experience and volunteer abroad. Brno is one of the most popular Erasmus+ destinations in Central Europe.

Who can participate?

Students registered in higher education institutions holding an Erasmus Charter for Higher Education

Duration

Study periods abroad last from 3 to 12 months (or 2 to 12 months for traineeships)

Financial Support

Monthly EU grants to help cover living costs in another country (amount varies by country)

How to Apply for Erasmus+

Follow these steps to start your Erasmus+ adventure in Brno

1

Check Eligibility

Verify with your home university that they have an Erasmus+ agreement with a Brno institution and that you meet all requirements.

2

Prepare Documents

Gather necessary documents including Learning Agreement, Transcript of Records, CV, Motivation Letter, and Language Certificate.

3

Submit Application

Apply through your home university's International Office by their deadline (typically 6-9 months before exchange).
